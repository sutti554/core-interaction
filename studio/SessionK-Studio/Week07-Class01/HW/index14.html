SPIDER

Artist: @adam_tattooer
Studio: Aviary Studio
Cost: $150
Bodypart: Above the knee
Year: 2020

Description
This tattoo is meant to remind me to be strong and fearless. I have never bee afraid of spiders and they represent my inner strength. I have always enjoyed silhouettes and this is my first blacked-out tattoo. A visiting artist came to Bandit Studio and I impulsively made an appointment. The artist, Adam, is only a bit older than me and it was such a unique experience having someone so close in age talk to me during the process. Most people I have been tattooed by are older than me by a good bit which I never really noticed until I had someone so young tattooing me.
