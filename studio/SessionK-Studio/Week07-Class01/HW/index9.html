OLIVE BRANCH

Artist: @nataletattoo
Studio: Have Fun Be Lucky Tattoo
Cost: $250
Bodypart: Ankle
Year: 2018

Description
This was my first ever tattoo and I got it as soon as I turned 18. I grew up being a big fan of tattoos as my uncle was covered and my brother had some too. As an artistic person, tattoos are not only art to me but a form of self-expression. To me, tattoos are my documentation of my values and an everyday reminder of what I believe in. As most people know, an olive branch represents peace and for me, I have always heavily identified with this and non-confrontation. It is a reminder to live life in a relaxed way and stay true to who I am. The experience was meaningful to me as becoming an adult and being bold. I did not care what anyone would think of it because the tattoo is for me and no one else.
