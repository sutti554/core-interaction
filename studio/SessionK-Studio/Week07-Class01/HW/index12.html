PALM

Artist: @tinedefiore
Studio: Black Oak Tattoo
Cost: $20 flash
Bodypart: Ankle
Year: 2019

Description
This was my first ever flash tattoo and is how I discovered Bandit Studio. One of my all-time favorite artists is Tine and I had been following her work for a long time. She works in Chicago so it would be a very expensive trip just to get a tattoo by her. BUT, she came to New York for a flash day and I got the opportunity to get something done by her. Although it is very small, it means so much to me to have at least a little ink by my favorite artist. One day I will get the money to make the trip to get something big done by her but for now, it was totally worth it.
